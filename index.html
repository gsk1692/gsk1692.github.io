Hello There

My name is Gokul S Krishnan. I am currently working as a Research Scientist at the Robert Bosch Centre for Data Science and Artificial Intelligence, Indian Institute of Technology Madras (IITM), Chennai. Prior to this, I worked as a Postdoctoral Fellow at RBCDSAI itself, under the esteemed mentorship of Prof. Balaraman Ravindran and Dr. Gitakrishnan Ramadurai.

I am currently working on multiple projects in the areas of Natural Language Processing, Machine/Deep Learning, Responsible AI and Public Health.

Prior to this, I worked as a Data Scientist in Penzigo Technology Solutions Pvt. Ltd., NITK-STEP, Surathkal, Mangaluru, INDIA.

I pursued my Ph.D. in Department of Information Technology, specializing in Healthcare Analytics and Informatics in the National Institute of Technology Karnataka, Surathkal, INDIA. I submitted by thesis in March 2020 and I cleared my defence viva in September 2020.

For my Ph.D., I worked under the eminent guidance of Dr. Sowmya Kamath S and I am a part of the Healthcare Analytics and Language Engineering (HALE) Lab headed by her at the department.

My research interests lie in the areas of Semantic Web, Information Retrieval, Knowledge and Data Sciences, Natural Language Processing, etc. I am a Python enthusiast and I love building Web & Mobile Apps.
