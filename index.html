Hello There

My name is Gokul S Krishnan. I am currently working as a Post Doctoral Researcher at the Robert Bosch Centre for Data Science and Artificial Intelligence, Indian Institute of Technology Madras (IITM), Chennai.

Prior to this, I worked as a Data Scientist in Penzigo Technology Solutions Pvt. Ltd., NITK-STEP, Surathkal, Mangaluru, INDIA.

I pursued my Ph.D. in Department of Information Technology, specializing in Healthcare Analytics and Informatics in the National Institute of Technology Karnataka, Surathkal, INDIA. I submitted by thesis in March 2020 and I am currently waiting for my thesis evaluation and defence viva.

I am working under the eminent guidance of Dr. Sowmya Kamath S and I am a part of the Healthcare Analytics and Language Engineering (HALE) Lab headed by her at the department.

My research interests lie in the areas of Semantic Web, Information Retrieval, Knowledge and Data Sciences, Natural Language Processing, etc. I am a Python enthusiast and I love building Web & Mobile Apps.
